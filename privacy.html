Ethics & Guidelines

Web Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 27, 2026. Learn how we handle clinical inquiries, diagnostic records, and cookie settings in compliance with medical confidentiality standards.

🫀 Fetal-Cardiovascular Confidentiality

At Shree Heart & Women Care Clinic, we hold clinical privacy in the highest regard. All cardiac records—including 12-lead electrocardiograms (ECG), echocardiographies (ECHO), fetal Doppler scans, and obstetric ultrasound files—are handled directly under standard clinical confidentiality rules in accordance with India's Digital Information Security in Healthcare (DISHA) and international health record standards.

We ensure that:

  • Diagnostic reports are handed over directly to the patient or verified family members.
  • Diagnostic results are not stored on public, unencrypted cloud environments.
  • Clinical discussions are confined strictly between Dr. Jwala or Dr. Sanjita and the patient.

📲 Form Inquiries & WhatsApp Integration

When you submit an appointment consultation form on our website, the details entered (including Name, Contact phone number, Consultation type, preferred date, slot timings, and voluntary medical notes) are not saved in a central web database.

Instead, these fields are processed entirely within your local browser to build a pre-filled WhatsApp message. Once you trigger submission, you are routed directly to the WhatsApp API to finalize the booking. This architecture ensures that your personal information remains strictly between you and our front clinical desk, preventing third-party access.

🍪 Local Storage Usage & Cookies

Our website utilizes light, non-tracking client-side browser features (cookies and localStorage) to improve your experience. These local variables do not track your browsing history or transmit your data back to us:

  • Theme Choice: Stores whether you prefer Dark Mode or Light Mode layouts to ensure visual consistency on subsequent page loads.
  • Cookie Consent: Remembers whether you have acknowledged the cookie banner popup so it does not appear repeatedly.
  • Chat State: Remembers current dialogue threads with Shree AI inside the active tab.

🤝 Your Control & Medical Ethics

As a patient, you retain absolute authority over your medical history and clinical reporting. You have the right to request a physical copy of all diagnostics performed on-site at Raja Bazar, Patna, or ask for digital records transmittal via secure, verified email.

For any privacy clarifications or concerns, you can contact our clinic directors directly at 9693762649.
