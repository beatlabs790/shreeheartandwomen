Our Origin Story

Dedicated to Heart Health and Maternal Wellness

Shree Heart & Women Care Clinic was founded with a singular, vital mission: to bridge the gap in specialized healthcare in Patna by bringing hospital-grade medical precision into a personalized and stress-free clinic setting.

We combine advanced diagnostic cardiology with highly specialized maternal-fetal medicine to support families at every milestone of life. Our clinics feature modern low-radiation equipment, relaxing interiors, and quick consultant queues, enabling patients to receive specialized attention without institutional delays.

Clinical Capability

Core Facilities & Technology

Our clinic is equipped with the latest diagnostic capabilities to ensure highly accurate results on-site.

Advanced Cardiology Suite

Designed for preventive and diagnostic clinical cardiology, featuring modern cardiac screening protocols:

  • High-Resolution Electrocardiogram (12-Lead ECG)
  • Transthoracic 2D Echocardiography (Echo)
  • Color Doppler Imaging for vascular mapping
  • Automated blood pressure & heart rate rhythm analysis

Maternal Care & Gynaecology Wing

Providing specialized services for comprehensive fetal-maternal well-being and high-risk pregnancies:

  • Obstetric and fetal ultrasound diagnostics
  • Continuous fetal heart rate Doppler monitoring
  • Prenatal screening and gestational risk evaluations
  • Comprehensive postpartum health consultations
Our History

Milestones of Excellence

How we grew to become one of Bailey Road's most trusted cardiac and women's health clinics.

2011

Clinic Foundation

Established Shree Heart & Women Care Clinic in Raja Bazar, Patna to serve as a focused outpatient clinic for cardiac and maternal consultations.

2015

Cardiology Upgrades

Integrated full-scale cardiac imaging and Doppler diagnostic technology, allowing patients to complete lab-grade tests instantly during consults.

2019

Fetal Medicine Focus

Launched the specialized Maternal-Fetal wellness module under Dr. Sanjita, specifically addressing high-risk gestational conditions.

2024

Digital Health Operations

Transitioned to modern electronic medical records, offering patients secure digital delivery of ECG and health reports via WhatsApp and email.
